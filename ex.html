Polytechnic

Polytechnic

If you're interested in technical education with practical skills, Polytechnic might be a great fit. Explore various specializations like Mechanical, Electrical, Civil, or Computer Engineering.

  • Future Scope: Direct entry into the workforce with a diploma in technical fields.
  • Possible Careers: Technician, Junior Engineer, Supervisor, Foreman

Diploma Courses

Diploma Courses

Diploma courses offer specialized training in fields like fashion design, hotel management, and more.

  • Future Scope: Enter the workforce with practical knowledge and skills.
  • Possible Careers: Fashion Designer, Hotel Manager, Graphic Designer, Chef

ITI (Industrial Training Institute)

ITI (Industrial Training Institute)

ITI courses are designed to provide hands-on skills in trades such as electrician, mechanic, and welder.

  • Future Scope: Skilled trades with direct employment opportunities.
  • Possible Careers: Electrician, Mechanic, Welder, Plumber

Other Options

Other Options

Consider other educational paths or vocational training based on your interests and career goals.

  • Vocational Courses: Training in specific job roles such as beautician, bakery, etc.
  • Distance Education: Study while working or pursuing other interests.
  • Entrepreneurship: Explore starting your own business if you have a passion or idea.
