Teacher Career Map

Completion of 10th Grade 🎓

Finish your 10th class with a focus on your favorite subjects, especially those you enjoy teaching.

Choose a relevant stream (Arts, Science, Commerce)📝

Opt for subjects that align with teaching, like English, Social Studies, or Science, depending on your interest.

Enroll in a Bachelor's Degree 🎒

Pursue a B.Ed. (Bachelor of Education) or a relevant degree like B.A. or B.Sc., depending on your teaching subject preference.

Complete a Teacher Training Program 🏫

Enroll in a teaching program or training to enhance your teaching skills. This could include certifications in Montessori, Primary, or High School teaching.

Consider a Master’s Degree 📚

If you aim to teach at higher levels or specialize, pursue a Master's in Education or your chosen subject.

Obtain necessary certifications ✅

Get certifications like CTET, TET, or other regional qualifications to become eligible for teaching positions.

Begin your teaching career 👩‍🏫

Start teaching at primary or secondary schools. You may start as an assistant teacher and gradually advance.

Engage in lifelong learning 📖

Keep attending workshops, seminars, and courses to stay updated with teaching methodologies and new educational trends.

Explore advanced roles 🌟

With experience, move into roles like Head of Department, Principal, or Educational Consultant.
